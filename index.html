This site is designed as a demo for using potree to share models of stratigraphy, take measurements and cut profile. The point cloud viewer is here. Use the 'scene' section of the menu to toggle the visibility of individual contexts. You will find basic measurement tools under the 'tools' section of the menu. Here you can measure angles and distances and draw profiles. The results will appear in the 'measurements' section of the menu.

Not all the contexts are present - just a sample to demonstrate how the viewer works.
